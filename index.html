Planning

The Plan is don't have a plan

Prepare my self to the unexpected worked better than to have a plan. Be able to face a problem was easier than to avoid them.

If you lose your wallet? If you lose your cards? If you lose your money? If you lose your passport? If you need some document?...

If you have to vote for an election? if you have to sign a contract in your contry? If you get sick or break anything?

Listing desired places looks easy until I realize that things can change. Would you thing you know good places better than locals?

If you don`t have a plan you can go anywhere but visa can be an obstacle.

Travel in the summer can be a good way to avoid bag but it can be expensive in some places.

Have a good shoes and take care of your feet. Have a good backpack and take care of your back.

KAYAK was cheaper than RTW ticket for me.

Places

First I wrote all the places I'd like to go, than I made some researches and took the wonders of the world as inspiration, which gave me around 104 places and finally I prioritize and put it in the map (Places worksheet of Planning excel).

Money

It took me around R125 hundred thousand of brazilian money but I planned it based on the worst case as if I had to travel only on expensive places.

Time

60 countries divided by 1 year and a half gave me almost 10 days for each country but I planned for any thing between 30 to 60 countries and from 1 year to 1 year and half.

Visas

There are visas required before arrival and visas that can be taken on arrival. From visas that are required before arrival they can be taken in another country or not and since they can expire, it may be a problem to consider togher with the time taken to be emitted.

I had to take the US, Canadian, Japonese and Australian visa before, Chinese in Thailand and those others I was able to handle it easily (Visa info worksheet of Planning excel).

Timezone & Weather

Follow the summer helped me to make my backpack small and travel the world from left to right helped me to get some extra time for each trip (Climate worksheet of Planning excel).

Carries and Health

Fishing line, a good tennis, a good backpack, first aid kit, raincoat, plastic bags, lighter, bowl, sleep bag, translater software, exchange rate software, offline maps, external hd, small notebook, international power adapters.

Dentist, Oculist, Checkup, Yellow fever vaccine, Health insurance. Exercise trains to do while travelling.

1 year of 3 hours of exercise every day after the trip. You never know when you will need to run away from a lion.
